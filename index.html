Simple Process

From Report to Resolution

A seamless journey ensuring every civic issue gets the attention it deserves

Report Issue

Citizens report civic problems through mobile app with photos, location, and description. Quick, easy, and intuitive interface.

Smart Routing

AI categorizes the issue type and automatically routes it to the appropriate government department for action.

Track Progress

Real-time updates show department assignment, officer responsibility, and action status. Complete transparency at every step.

Auto-Escalate

If departments don't respond within SLA timeframes, issues automatically escalate to elected representatives for intervention.

Verify Resolution

Departments upload proof of resolution with before/after photos. Citizens verify and confirm issue closure, ensuring accountability.

Community Impact

Resolved issues remain visible as success stories, building trust and encouraging more citizens to participate in civic engagement.
